Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment

You can book your appointment by contacting me directly via WhatsApp at +34 610 609 038 or via Instagram @toyooner. You can also use the contact form on this page. I will respond as soon as possible to coordinate the date and time.

The price varies depending on the size, complexity and estimated time of the design, I'll give you a personalized quote after seeing your idea and references.

Yes, a depositis required to book your appointment. This deposit is deducted from the final price of the tattoo. The deposit is non-refundable if cancelled with less than 48 hours notice.

Surface healing takes 2-3 weeks, but full healing can take up to 2-3 months. I will provide you with detailed aftercare instructions after your session. It is important to follow these instructions to ensure the best healing and final result.

Of course! You can bring your own ideas, references, or designs. I will work with you to adapt the design to your skin and ensure the result is perfect. I can also create custom designs from scratch based on your ideas.

Make sure to eat well before your appointment, stay hydrated, and get a good night's sleep. Avoid alcohol 24 hours prior. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area to be tattooed. If you have any medical conditions or take medication, please inform me in advance.

You can reschedule your appointment by giving at least 48 hours notice. If you cancel with less than 48 hours notice, the deposit will be non-refundable. I understand that unforeseen events may occur, so please contact me as soon as possible if you need to make changes.

I mostly work in black and gray, but if your design calls for it, I can add color. Every tattoo is unique—let’s talk about your idea and find the best way to bring it to life.
